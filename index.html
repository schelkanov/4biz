Traditionally, organizations had their development, testing and maintenance teams operate in silos. As limitations of this model surfaced and compelling benefits of adopting agile development methodology emerged, organizations started breaking down the silos to support greater collaboration between their development & operations teams, and increasingly looked at an extension of Agile, namely DevOps.

DevOps is an operational approach which integrates development & operations in the same cycle with greater emphasis on Continuous Integration, Infrastructure-as-code, Continuous Testing & Continuous Delivery to reduce the code-to-deployment time. DevOps standardizes efforts and ensures accelerated time to market, improved deployment quality and greater operational efficiency.

DevOps Cycle

1.Test Automation: OutForce proven Test Automation expertise across various platforms (Cloud & Mobile), technologies and domains coupled with our proprietary Test Automation Accelerator (ETAS) positions us uniquely to provide custom test automation solutions to kick-start DevOps cycle. OutForce proven test automation framework enables Automated Acceptance Testing in DevOps cycle. As a result brings in Agility to DevOps Testing cycle.

2.Virtualization: OutForce expertise in creating and configuring test environments for clients across business domains using Virtual Environments locally (like VSphere, HyperVisor, Virtual Box etc.) and on the cloud (like Amazon EC2 AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud etc.) gives us a proven edge to assist client’s DevOps implementation.

3.Continuous Integration: With rich experience in Continuous integration testing, Gallop’s CI testers are experts in configuration and execution of popular CI tools such as Agile GO, Cruise Control, Chef, Puppet, Microsoft TFS, Jenkins, Bamboo, Hudson and so on, helping client’s DevOps team.

4.Monitoring: Continuous monitoring is a pressing need of CI in DevOps. This translates to not just finding out the defects but also anticipating issues and providing live feedback during build monitoring. Gallop’s software testing expertise and robust reporting frameworks ensures setting up of client’s DevOps monitors for enhanced monitoring.
